Product Description:
EngageHub (EH) is a versatile employee engagement platform designed to foster collaboration, communication, and productivity within organizations. It offers features such as social networking, knowledge sharing, recognition programs, and feedback channels. EngageHub empowers employees, connects teams, and enhances overall engagement for a thriving workplace culture.

Problem Statement:
Organizations struggle with disengaged employees, siloed communication, and lack of opportunities for collaboration and recognition. Existing employee engagement solutions lack comprehensive features, user-friendly interfaces, and seamless integration, hindering employee satisfaction and productivity.
