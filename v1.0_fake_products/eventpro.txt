Product Description:
EventPro (EP) is a comprehensive event management software designed to streamline the planning, organization, and execution of events. It offers features such as attendee registration, venue management, agenda scheduling, and communication tools. With EventPro, event planners can deliver seamless and memorable experiences to their attendees.

Problem Statement:
Event planners face challenges in managing and coordinating various aspects of events, including attendee registration, schedule conflicts, and communication gaps. Existing event management solutions lack flexibility, scalability, and robust features, resulting in inefficiencies and subpar event experiences.
