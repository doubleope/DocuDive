Product Description:
SalesBoost (SB) is a comprehensive sales enablement software designed to empower sales teams and drive revenue growth. It offers features such as content management, sales training, performance analytics, and CRM integration. SalesBoost equips sales professionals with the tools and knowledge they need to succeed, enhancing their productivity and effectiveness.

Problem Statement:
Sales teams often struggle with sales enablement, including accessing relevant content, staying updated on product knowledge, and measuring performance. Existing sales enablement solutions lack integration, personalization, and actionable insights, resulting in missed opportunities and subpar sales performance.
