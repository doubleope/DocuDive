Product Description:
TaskFlow (TF) is a streamlined workflow management software designed to simplify task delegation, tracking, and collaboration. It offers intuitive task boards, progress visualization, team communication features, and integrations with popular productivity tools. TaskFlow helps teams stay organized, enhance productivity, and achieve efficient workflow management.

Problem Statement:
Teams often struggle with managing workflows, including task assignment, progress tracking, and communication gaps. Existing workflow management solutions lack user-friendliness, flexibility, and real-time collaboration, leading to inefficiencies, missed deadlines, and decreased productivity.

Elevator Pitch:
Introducing TaskFlow (TF), the ultimate solution for effortless workflow management. Streamline your tasks, boost team productivity, and achieve seamless collaboration. Visualize workflows with intuitive task boards (TB), track progress in real-time (PR), and communicate effectively within your team (CT).
