Product Description:
TaskMaster (TM) is a powerful task management software designed to help individuals and teams stay organized, prioritize tasks, and achieve their goals efficiently. It offers features such as task tracking, collaboration tools, reminders, and progress monitoring. TaskMaster empowers users to streamline their workflows, increase productivity, and accomplish more.

Problem Statement:
Individuals and teams often struggle with managing multiple tasks, staying organized, and tracking progress effectively. Existing task management solutions are often complex, lack collaboration features, and fail to provide a seamless user experience, leading to inefficiencies and missed deadlines.
