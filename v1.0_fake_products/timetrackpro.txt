Product Description:
TimeTrackPro (TTP) is a comprehensive time tracking software designed to help individuals and teams monitor and manage their time effectively. It offers features such as time logging, project tracking, reporting, and integration with productivity tools. TimeTrackPro enables users to gain insights into their time usage, improve productivity, and optimize resource allocation.

Problem Statement:
People often struggle with tracking their time accurately, managing multiple projects, and identifying time-wasting activities. Existing time tracking solutions lack ease of use, comprehensive reporting, and integration capabilities, leading to inefficient time management and lower productivity.
